Tacos Recipe

Description

Tacos are a beloved Mexican dish featuring a folded or rolled tortilla filled with a variety of ingredients. Traditionally made with corn or flour tortillas, they can be filled with seasoned meats, beans, cheese, vegetables, and fresh toppings like salsa, guacamole, and cilantro.

Tacos are versatile and customizable, allowing for endless combinations to suit different tastes. Served as a casual street food or a festive meal, they are enjoyed worldwide for their bold flavors and satisfying crunch, making them a favorite for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • tortillas (corn or flour)
  • ground beef, chicken, pork, or other protein (or beans for a vegetarian option)
  • taco seasoning (or spices like cumin, chili powder, and paprika)
  • cheese (cheddar, or queso fresco)
  • lettuce (shredded)
  • tomatoes (diced)
  • onion (diced)
  • salsa
  • guacamole
  • sour cream
  • fresh cilantro (for garnish)
  • lime wedges (for serving)

Steps

  1. Cook Protein: Brown ground beef, chicken, or pork in a skillet. Drain fat.
  2. Add Seasoning: Stir in taco seasoning and a bit of water. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Prepare Toppings: Chop lettuce, tomatoes, and onion. Grate or crumble cheese.
  4. Warm Tortillas: Heat tortillas in a skillet or microwave.
  5. Assemble Tacos: Place the cooked protein on each tortilla. Add lettuce, tomatoes, onion, and cheese.
  6. Add Sauces: Top with salsa, guacamole, and sour cream.
  7. Garnish: Finish with cilantro and a squeeze of lime.
  8. Serve: Enjoy your tacos!
